BL-BENLORG LIMITED COMPANY

BL-Benlorg limited company is a limited liability company based in Ghana with a high sense of duty to serve in areas of Engineering, Transportation, Mining and Precious Minerals trading. Having worked in the above stated industries for over eight (8) years, we can clearly confirm that we have the capacity to deliver to any of our existing and new clients the best of world class service in the above stated industries.

What We Do

Haulage,Hiring of Earth moving Equipment (eg.t rucks), Precious Mineral Trading and Small Scale Mining.

Haulage

Benlorg provides a world class haulage service to mining and construction firms in western region and the other parts of Ghana. Transportation of Goods from port to ware houses and ware houses to port are of no exception. We deliver on time and always make sure that delivered goods are intact. This means that for efficient and better transport services Benlorg will always be the preferred choice for our mining and engineering partners.

Small Scale Mining

Benlorg having had the machinery for small scale mining provides to our concession partners the needed machinery that meet their productivity targets. Our small scale mining partners under our mutual partnership agreement give us the opportunity to buy their Gold at a competitive price and retail it to our International partners.

Precious Mineral Trading

BL-Benlorg Limited company, deals in the trading of precious minerals such as Gold and Diamond. Over the years Benlorg limited company operating with the confines of the o the mineral acts of Ghana have helped in the purchase of large volumes of Gold and diamonds and retailed it to international buyers and agents. Have acquired permits and licensed for this business, our international buying agents have no fear when it comes to doing business. Are precious minerals sold to international trade partners also go through all the pretesting and refinery processes. By this our clients shall be assured of good & quality mineral.

Hiring of Trucks & Earth Moving Equipment

Having worked with allied mining and construction industries for some years, Benlorg offers modern and fuel-efficient machinery for rental and sale purposes to clients. Understanding the production targets by the mining and construction industries has helped us to provide to those in the industry highly efficient machinery which are durable and are also less costly to maintain. In construction we have the capacity to provide all types of heavy-duty machines used in both Civil and Structural (Apartment) engineering. Our machines are also given to clients on a competitive price rate that help cut their budgets and maximize their profits.
